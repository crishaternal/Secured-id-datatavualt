How It Works
Three Steps to Total Privacy

SecureID wraps your identity in an encrypted reference layer. You share a code — never your real data.

01
🔐

Create Your Vault

Register once. Store your name, address, contact details, and payment info inside an AES-256 encrypted personal vault tied to your SecureID account.

02
🔑

Generate an STR Code

Every transaction gets its own unique Security Transaction Reference — a short code that represents your data without ever exposing it to the other party.

03
✅

Share the Code, Not You

Merchants, platforms, and services verify your identity through the STR system. If a site gets hacked, they only have a useless reference code — your real data stays safe.

04
📊

Monitor & Revoke

See every time your STR is queried in your dashboard. Revoke any reference instantly if you suspect misuse — zero impact on your other transactions.
